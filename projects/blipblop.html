Blipp blopp

Blipp blopp is an application that focus on the relationship between grandparents and their grandchildren.
In blipp blopp you can play games between each other, chat och send pictures. The pictures can be modified to diffrent puzzels and you can change the color of them.
There is one more function where you can play a ping-pong game. It starts with a white picture and together you can colorize the picture and at the end of the game you can see the original picture.
