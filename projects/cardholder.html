Cardholder

Intro

During the Visual Communications course we got an assignment to create an App that used wearable technologies. The task were to choose an existing wearable technology and conceptualize a practical application for it.

Concept

I decided to create a concept that took all of a user's membership cards and stored them all in one place, easily accessible through the user's phone. This allows for the user to not worry about carrying around all of their cards in their wallet, since most of them are commonly not used every day.

Process

The first step was to create the concept and to sketch it out. I used sketching and storyboarding as tools to design and communicate my concept in a clear way while leaving room for further iteration. After that, I created a storyboard with Adobe Photoshop to display the flow of the application, and what the thought area of use is. The last step of this project was to create interface mock-ups with Adobe Illustrator.
